About Project

Daksin.ai is designed as a research support space that combines academic literacy, publication ethics, and responsible AI assistance.

Background

Many promising research ideas need guidance before they can become structured, credible, and ethically publishable academic manuscripts.

What It Does

The platform supports brainstorming, academic writing, citation checking, and publication preparation through a practical and easy-to-follow workflow.

Purpose

Its purpose is to help researchers, educators, and students produce clearer, more relevant, transparent, and academically responsible research outputs.

Developer

Daksin.ai is part of Daksinapati Projects, developed within the PTIK environment at Universitas Negeri Jakarta.
